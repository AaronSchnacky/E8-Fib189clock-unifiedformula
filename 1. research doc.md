* The specific μ companion tower, proton anchor index 113, residuals 3594/6456, and correction magnitudes reflect numerical optimization rather than pure deduction from the axioms.

Future refinement could seek stricter lattice-based origins for indices/exponents/corrections or focus on the qubit predictions as the decisive test.
